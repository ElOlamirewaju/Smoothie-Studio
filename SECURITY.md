# Security Policy

## Supported Versions
We support the latest main branch and the most recent tagged release (if applicable).

## Reporting a Vulnerability
- Please email security reports to security@smoothiestudio.dev. Avoid posting publicly in issues or discussions.
- Include steps to reproduce, affected areas, and any potential impact youâ€™ve identified.
- We will acknowledge receipt within 5 business days and aim to provide an initial assessment or remediation plan within 10 business days.

## Preferred Practices
- Limit proof-of-concept exploits to the minimum required to demonstrate impact.
- If you find exposed credentials or secrets, do not use them; report the location and type.
- For UI security/privacy concerns, include screenshots or a short clip where helpful.
